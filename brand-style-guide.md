## Brand Essence & Voice
- **Essence:** A scientific ethereal medspa that elevates your inner beauty to a heavenly glow.
- **Tagline:** “Award Winning Medical Aesthetics”
- **Personality:** Sophisticated, nurturing, empowering.
- **Tone:** Inspirational, benefit-focused, warm. Use transformative phrases like “Elevate Radiance” and “Unlock Aura” to signal confidence.

## Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Primary Navy | #0F1D2C | Headlines, backgrounds, key text |
| Accent Gold | #F3D6BE | Highlights, calls to action, badges |
| Light Background | #FAF8F5 | Page background, airy sections |
| Pure White | #FFFFFF | Text on dark backgrounds, negative space |
| Text Dark | #2A2A2A | Body copy on light backgrounds |

**Pro Tip:** Pair Navy with Gold for rich contrast; lean on Light Background to keep layouts uncluttered and refined.

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Montserrat (400, 600, 700) for body text, subheadings, and buttons.
- **Secondary Font:** Playfair Display (Regular, Bold) for hero headlines and major section titles.
- **Fallbacks:** Arial or Helvetica for Montserrat; Georgia for Playfair Display.

**Tip:** Use Playfair Display for an editorial hero moment while Montserrat maintains modern clarity elsewhere.

## Iconography & Imagery
1. **Angel Wings & Halo**
   - Symbolize divine transformation with an ethereal, uplifting vibe.
   - Keep them subtle—low opacity behind key content or headings.
2. **Lotus Flower**
   - Represents rebirth, purity, and renewal. Ideal for emblems or membership materials.
   - Favor simplified line art or minimalist silhouettes to match the modern-luxe aesthetic.
3. **Photography**
   - High-key, softly lit imagery of confident individuals with glowing skin.
   - Neutral or airy backgrounds that complement the palette.

## Signature Look & Feel
- **Layout & Spacing:** Generous margins and 1.5–2x line height for an upscale, spa-like ambiance.
- **Corners:** Rounded edges (~15px) on cards/boxes for modern softness.
- **Shadows & Glows:** Subtle drop shadows (e.g., `0 10px 30px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)`) and radial glows for halo/lotus highlights.

## CTA & Button Styles
- **Primary CTA:** Navy background, white text, bold Montserrat 600/700, pill shape (border-radius ~30px), hover lift with soft shadow or `transform: translateY(-3px)`.
- **Secondary CTA:** Accent Gold background, navy text, reserved for membership sign-ups, promotions, or special offers.
